Part 3
Part 3 combines Part 2 and Part 1
Using the Environment from Part 1 we imported the asset UnityChan from Part 2
Right click to (un)select UnityChan or the Obstacles 
The Obstacles are controlled using w,a,s,d
UnityChan is controlled by left clicking an area
When a left click is issued on an area, UnityChan will attempt to walk to that spot
If a left click is issued on UnityChan, she will start sprinting.
To stop her from sprinting, left click her again.
When traversing off mesh links, UnityChan will Jump!
